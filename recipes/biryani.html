Biryani

Description

Biryani? It's the king of rice dishes in South Asia. Picture this: flavorful meat marinated in spices, layered with partly cooked rice, then cooked together till it's a fragrant explosion in your mouth. Spices like cumin, coriander, and cardamom give it a kick. There are tons of regional variations, each claiming to be the best. It's rich, aromatic, and downright addictive. And don't forget the sides like raita and salad. It's not just a dish; it's an experience.
