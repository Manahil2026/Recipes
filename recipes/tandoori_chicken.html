Tandoori Chicken

Description

Tandoori chicken: it's the bomb. Picture juicy chicken, marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices that'll knock your socks off. Then it's skewered and blasted in a clay oven until it's smoky, tender, and downright addictive. It's spicy, it's tangy, and it's guaranteed to make your taste buds dance. Serve it up with some naan and chutney, and you've got yourself a meal fit for a king.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger, garlic
  • Spices: Cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, chili powder
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice or vinegar (optional)

Steps

  1. Marinate: Combine yogurt, ginger, garlic, spices, salt, and optional lemon juice/vinegar. Coat chicken pieces and marinate for a few hours or overnight.
  2. Preheat: Preheat your oven to high heat (around 450°F or 230°C).
  3. Skewer: Thread marinated chicken onto skewers or place on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake: Bake chicken in the preheated oven until cooked through and charred on the edges, typically around 25-30 minutes.
  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and lemon wedges. Enjoy hot with naan bread and chutney!
